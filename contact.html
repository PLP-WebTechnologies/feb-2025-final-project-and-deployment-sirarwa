Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our repair services

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen replacements and battery replacements are completed within 1-2 hours. More complex repairs may take up to 24 hours. We'll provide you with an estimated completion time when you drop off your device.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes, all our repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labor. If you experience any issues with the repaired component within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize wait times. You can book an appointment through our contact form or by calling us directly.

Will my data be preserved during repair?

We make every effort to preserve your data during repairs. However, we always recommend backing up your device before bringing it in for service, as some repairs may require a factory reset or could potentially result in data loss.
