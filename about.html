About Us

Our Story

SirFixit was founded in 2025 aiming to provide fast, reliable, and affordable mobile device repairs without compromising on quality.

What started as a small repair shop has grown into a trusted service center with a reputation for excellence. We've helped thousands of customers get their devices back in working order, often saving them from costly replacements.

Our team consists of certified technicians with years of experience in repairing all types of mobile devices. We continuously update our skills and knowledge to keep up with the latest technology trends and repair techniques.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day

Our Mission

To provide exceptional repair services that extend the life of mobile devices, reduce electronic waste, and save our customers money while delivering a hassle-free experience.

  • Innovation: Staying current with the latest repair techniques
